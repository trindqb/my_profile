About me

My name is Tri Nguyen Dang (Nguyễn Đăng Trị in Vietnamese).

I was born in Quang Binh, Viet Nam. I received the B.E. degree in Information for Education from Hue University, College of Education in 2014, and the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Kyung Hee University, Seoul, Korea in 2023. I received several best paper awards from Korea Computer Congress(KCC), and Korea Software Congress (KIISE). In 2013, I received the Consolation Prize of Olympiad in ACM ICPC Vietnam. My research interests include network economics, game theory, network optimization, wireless network, communication network, multi-access edge computing, cloud computing, fog computing, infotainment services in mobile networks, device-to-device communication, vehicle-to-vehicle communication, deep reinforcement learning, federated learning for 5G and beyond.
